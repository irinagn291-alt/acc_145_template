PageTurner is a reading tracker for book lovers who want to build a consistent reading habit. Log pages read each day, organize by bookshelf setup, and see how your reading rhythm changes over time.

KEY FEATURES
• Log daily pages in a few taps
• Configure bookshelf setup to match your reading life
• Streak-focused dashboard to keep momentum visible
• Agenda-style calendar for day-by-day reading review
• Page-based entry flow designed for quick logging
• Horizontal bar charts, grouped history, and Insights
• Best Reading Day and Reading Forecast
• Export your reading log to CSV
• Light, Dark, and System appearance modes
• Optional haptic feedback

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
PageTurner stores your reading history locally on your device. No account, no cloud sync, no ads, and no tracking.

Whether you are finishing one book or juggling several, PageTurner helps you stay accountable and celebrate progress.
